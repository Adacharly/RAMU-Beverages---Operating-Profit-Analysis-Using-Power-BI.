# Business Introduction

RAMU Beverages is a regional distributor and supplier of Coca-Cola beverage products operating across multiple U.S. locations. The company manages sales, distribution, and profitability across several beverage brands, including Coca-Cola, Dasani Water, Sprite, Fanta, Powerade, and Diet Coke.

As operations expanded across regions and product lines, RAMU Beverages required deeper visibility into profitability drivers, beverage performance, and geographic sales distribution to support strategic decision-making and financial planning.

---

# Problem Statement

Prior to this analysis, RAMU Beverages faced several operational and financial challenges:

- Limited insight into operating profit by beverage brand  
- No clear understanding of profitability drivers  
- Lack of regional sales visibility across operating states  
- Absence of centralized quarterly financial reporting  
- Difficulty identifying high-margin vs low-margin products  

Without an integrated analytical view, leadership could not effectively optimize product mix, regional strategy, or profit performance.

# Recommendations

## Product Strategy
- Prioritize Coca-Cola and Dasani in promotions and distribution.
- Expand availability of high-margin beverages in top-selling regions.
- Evaluate growth strategies for lower-profit brands like Fanta.

## Regional Strategy
- Increase marketing investment in high-density sales states.
- Analyze underperforming regions for expansion opportunities.
- Align distribution routes with demand clusters.

## Profit Optimization
- Promote high-margin beverage mix.
- Monitor pricing strategy to maintain margins.
- Replicate successful Coca-Cola sales strategies across brands.

## Reporting & Analytics
- Maintain Power BI dashboard as executive decision tool.
- Track quarterly profit and sales trends.
- Integrate store-level profitability analysis.
